Learn a little bit about me.


Originally from south Alabama, Matthew Johnson has been living off and on in the Seattle area since 2007, settling down for good in Columbia City in 2012. He has a career background in Communications. Most recently he was in charge of Rider Communications for Sound Transit.


Matthew has a Bachelors of Science in International Studies. He is also a graduate of the Pyschological Operations Non-Commissioned Officers Course at the U.S. Army John F Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School.


Looking to stay in a Seattle area experiencing rapidly increasing costs of living, Matthew has enrolled in Code Fellows to become a software developer. It is his hope to work for Blue Origin but in reality he will work for whoever will pay him enough and where he doesn't have to drive.
